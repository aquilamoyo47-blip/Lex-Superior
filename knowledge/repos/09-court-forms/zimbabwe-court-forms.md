# Zimbabwe High Court — Precedent Court Forms and Pleadings
## Standard Forms for Civil Proceedings

### Form 1: Summons (Simple Summons)

**IN THE HIGH COURT OF ZIMBABWE**
**HELD AT [PLACE]**
**CASE NO.: HC [NUMBER]/[YEAR]**

**BETWEEN:**

[NAME OF PLAINTIFF] ... PLAINTIFF

**AND**

[NAME OF DEFENDANT] ... DEFENDANT

**SUMMONS**

To the above-mentioned defendant:

YOU ARE HEREBY COMMANDED to appear before the High Court of Zimbabwe, [Place], on [DATE] at [TIME], to answer the claim of the plaintiff as set out below, and if you fail to do so, judgment may be given against you.

**CLAIM:**
The plaintiff claims payment of the sum of ZWL/USD [AMOUNT] being money due and owing by the defendant to the plaintiff in respect of [CAUSE OF ACTION].

TAKE NOTICE that the defendant is required to enter an appearance to defend within [10/21/30] days after service of this summons.

*Issued at [Place] on [Date]*

___________________________
REGISTRAR / PLAINTIFF'S ATTORNEY

### Form 3: Declaration

**IN THE HIGH COURT OF ZIMBABWE**
**HELD AT [PLACE]**
**CASE NO.: HC [NUMBER]/[YEAR]**

**BETWEEN:**

[PLAINTIFF] ... PLAINTIFF

**AND**

[DEFENDANT] ... DEFENDANT

**DECLARATION**

1. The plaintiff is [NAME], [DESCRIPTION], with its/his/her principal place of business/residence at [ADDRESS].

2. The defendant is [NAME], [DESCRIPTION], with its/his/her principal place of business/residence at [ADDRESS], and is properly before this Honourable Court.

3. [STATEMENT OF FACTS GIVING RISE TO CAUSE OF ACTION]

4. [LEGAL BASIS OF CLAIM]

5. [AMOUNT CLAIMED / RELIEF SOUGHT]

**WHEREFORE** the plaintiff respectfully prays for an order in the following terms:

(a) Payment by the defendant to the plaintiff of the sum of ZWL/USD [AMOUNT];
(b) Interest on the aforesaid sum at the rate of [X]% per annum from [DATE] to date of payment;
(c) Costs of suit;
(d) Such further and/or alternative relief as the Honourable Court deems fit.

Dated at [Place] this [Day] day of [Month] [Year].

___________________________
PLAINTIFF'S LEGAL PRACTITIONER
[FIRM NAME]

---

### Form 4: Plea

**IN THE HIGH COURT OF ZIMBABWE**
**HELD AT [PLACE]**
**CASE NO.: HC [NUMBER]/[YEAR]**

**BETWEEN:**

[PLAINTIFF] ... PLAINTIFF

**AND**

[DEFENDANT] ... DEFENDANT

**PLEA**

**SPECIAL PLEA** *(if applicable)*

1. The defendant pleads specially that the plaintiff's claim has prescribed, in that—
   (a) the debt became due on [DATE];
   (b) more than three years have elapsed since the debt became due;
   (c) the running of prescription was not interrupted or delayed;
   (d) the debt is accordingly extinguished by prescription in terms of section 11 of the Prescription Act [Chapter 8:11].

**IN THE ALTERNATIVE — PLEA ON THE MERITS**

2. Paragraphs 1 and 2 of the declaration are admitted.

3. Paragraph 3 of the declaration is denied. The defendant's version is as follows: [ALTERNATIVE VERSION OF FACTS].

4. The defendant accordingly denies that the plaintiff is entitled to the relief claimed.

**WHEREFORE** the defendant prays for the dismissal of the plaintiff's claim with costs.

Dated at [Place] this [Day] day of [Month] [Year].

___________________________
DEFENDANT'S LEGAL PRACTITIONER
[FIRM NAME]

### Form 5: Notice of Motion

**IN THE HIGH COURT OF ZIMBABWE**
**HELD AT [PLACE]**
**CASE NO.: HC [NUMBER]/[YEAR]**

**In the matter of:**

[NAME OF APPLICANT] ... APPLICANT

**AND**

[NAME OF RESPONDENT] ... RESPONDENT

**NOTICE OF MOTION**

TAKE NOTICE that the Applicant will make application to this Honourable Court on [DATE] at [TIME] or as soon thereafter as the matter may be heard, for an Order in the following terms:

1. [PRAYER 1]
2. [PRAYER 2]
3. Costs of the application;
4. Such further and/or alternative relief as the Honourable Court deems fit.

TAKE NOTICE FURTHER that the affidavit of [DEPONENT], attached hereto and marked "Annexure A", together with supporting documents, will be used in support of the application.

TAKE NOTICE FURTHER that any party wishing to oppose this application is required to notify the applicant and to file an opposing affidavit within [10] days of service hereof.

Dated at [Place] this [Day] day of [Month] [Year].

___________________________
APPLICANT'S LEGAL PRACTITIONER
[FIRM NAME]

### Form 6: Founding Affidavit Structure

**FOUNDING AFFIDAVIT**

I, the undersigned,

**[FULL NAME OF DEPONENT]**

do hereby make oath and state:

1. I am an adult [male/female], [occupation], employed by / residing at [ADDRESS]. I am the [Applicant / duly authorised representative of the Applicant].

2. I am duly authorised to depose to this affidavit on behalf of the Applicant [by virtue of resolution attached hereto as Annexure "A1"].

3. The facts herein deposed to fall within my personal knowledge and are true and correct to the best of my knowledge and belief.

4. **[BACKGROUND]**: [Set out the background facts]

5. **[GROUNDS FOR RELIEF]**: [Set out the legal and factual basis for the order sought]

6. **[URGENCY]** *(if applicable)*: I submit that this matter is urgent for the following reasons: [Set out grounds of urgency]

7. I respectfully request this Honourable Court to grant the relief set out in the notice of motion.

**DEPONENT**

Sworn before me at [Place] on [Date].

___________________________
COMMISSIONER OF OATHS

---

### Form 7: Heads of Argument — Structure

**IN THE HIGH COURT OF ZIMBABWE**
**HELD AT [PLACE]**
**CASE NO.: HC [NUMBER]/[YEAR]**

**BETWEEN:**

[APPLICANT/PLAINTIFF] ... APPLICANT/PLAINTIFF

**AND**

[RESPONDENT/DEFENDANT] ... RESPONDENT/DEFENDANT

**APPLICANT'S/PLAINTIFF'S HEADS OF ARGUMENT**

**A. INTRODUCTION**

1. This is an application / action for [state nature of proceedings].

**B. BACKGROUND FACTS**

2. [Numbered paragraphs setting out facts]

**C. LEGAL ISSUES**

3. The following legal issues arise for determination:
   (a) [Issue 1]
   (b) [Issue 2]

**D. SUBMISSIONS**

*Issue 1: [State Issue]*

4. It is submitted that [legal proposition]. The authority for this proposition is [case / statute / rule].

*Issue 2: [State Issue]*

5. [Continue numbered submissions]

**E. CONCLUSION**

6. For the aforesaid reasons, the Applicant/Plaintiff respectfully submits that:
   (a) [Relief 1]
   (b) [Relief 2]

**Table of Cases:**
1. [Case name] (citation) — para [X]

**Table of Statutes:**
1. [Statute name] — para [X]

___________________________
COUNSEL FOR APPLICANT/PLAINTIFF
[NAME OF LEGAL PRACTITIONER]
