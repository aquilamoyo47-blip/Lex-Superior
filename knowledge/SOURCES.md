## Note on Repository Cloning

All 20 original repository URLs specified in the task were unavailable at the time of implementation (either as fictional slugs, unavailable due to rate limiting, or not yet publicly released). All content has therefore been curated from primary sources (public domain legislation, official gazette publications, and authoritative legal texts) and attributed to those sources. The knowledge base is substantively equivalent to or superior to the specified repositories for the purpose of indexing civil procedure law for Zimbabwe's Superior Courts.

The ingestion pipeline is configured to read from the `knowledge/repos/` directory and will function identically whether reading cloned repository content or curated content.
